How it works

  • Setup
  • Build & Deploy

Setup

Get started by downloading our cli to create a stack. It will spin up a Kubernetes cluster and other resources in your GCP account.

You can deploy multiple apps in a cluster and share services among them.

Build & Deploy

Build your apps and deploy them to cluster. You can also -

  • Create 12-factor apps with Buildpacks, Dockerfile or docker-compose.yml on Kubernetes.
  • Provision infrastructure with helm charts managed by us.
  • Link GCP services easily (like automatic cloud-sql proxy, service-account and many more).
